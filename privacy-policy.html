Privacy Policy for Interactive Dreams Games

Interactive Dreams is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how our mobile games collect, use, and share information. By installing and playing our games, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Interactive Dreams does not collect any personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address
  • Contacts
  • Precise location
  • Photos, media, or files
  • User accounts or login information
  • User-generated content such as chat or messages

We do not operate our own servers to store user data.

2. Information Collected Automatically by Third-Party Services

Our games include third-party SDKs that may automatically collect certain device and app-related information for ads, analytics, crash reporting, and performance optimization.

A. Advertising Networks

We use the following ad networks directly or via AppLovin MAX mediation:

  • Google AdMob
  • AppLovin MAX
  • Meta Audience Network
  • Mintegral
  • Liftoff / Vungle
  • Unity Ads
  • InMobi

These networks may collect:

  • Advertising ID / device identifiers
  • Approximate location (IP-based)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • App usage and interactions
  • Ad impressions, clicks, and performance data
  • Fraud detection signals and diagnostics

B. Analytics & Performance Services

We use the following analytics services:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • GameAnalytics

These services may collect:

  • Gameplay events and level progress
  • Session length and engagement
  • Crash logs and performance diagnostics
  • Device and system information

3. Children's Privacy

Our games are intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us and we will work with our partners to remove it.

4. How Data Is Used

Data collected by third-party SDKs is used for:

  • Delivering and optimizing advertisements
  • Tracking performance and analytics
  • Crash reporting and stability improvement
  • Preventing fraud and abuse
  • Enhancing user experience

Interactive Dreams does not sell any user data.

5. Data Sharing

Automatically collected data may be shared with trusted partners (ad networks and analytics providers) solely for operational purposes. No personally identifiable information is shared.

6. Security

All third-party services use industry-standard security including HTTPS encryption. Interactive Dreams does not store or process personal data on its own servers.

7. Your Choices

Depending on your region and device:

  • You can reset or disable the Advertising ID in device settings.
  • GDPR regions may see a consent dialog for personalized ads.
  • Some devices allow disabling analytics in OS privacy settings.

8. Third-Party Privacy Policies

For more information, you can review the privacy policies of our partners:

  • AppLovin
  • Unity Ads
  • Meta Audience Network
  • Mintegral
  • InMobi
